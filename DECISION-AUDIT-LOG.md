## Decision #001 — Tagline Pivot
- **Date:** 2026-03-27
- **Decision:** Change tagline from "Verified from Every Angle" to "Value from Every Angle"
- **Rationale:** The original tagline emphasized verification/authentication only. The company's strategy now encompasses three dimensions of value creation (fractional ownership, tokenization, lending), not just verification. "Value" captures the full scope.
- **Impact:** Updated across: landing page hero, metadata, OG tags, Twitter cards, CSS design tokens, all future marketing materials
- **Decided by:** Shane Pierson

## Decision #002 — Three-Path Value Creation Strategy
- **Date:** 2026-03-27
- **Decision:** Expand from tokenization-only to three value creation paths: Fractional Securities, Tokenization, and Debt Instruments
- **Rationale:** A single verified, vaulted gemstone can generate value through multiple structures simultaneously. Fractional securities lower access barriers, tokenization enables programmable compliance and liquidity, and debt instruments let holders unlock capital without selling. This positions PleoChrome as a full-spectrum value orchestrator rather than a single-product company.
- **Impact:** New landing page feature section, four new strategy documents (343KB), updated all research context. Revenue stacking analysis shows Path B+C on same asset = $1.12M/5yr vs $745K standalone.
- **Decided by:** Shane Pierson

## Decision #003 — Platform Agnosticism (Remove Brickken Commitment)
- **Date:** 2026-03-27
- **Decision:** Remove Brickken as the stated tokenization partner. Position PleoChrome as platform-agnostic, evaluating multiple providers.
- **Rationale:** (a) Zoniqx call on 2026-03-26 revealed a compelling alternative with Chainlink PoR already integrated and physical asset experience. (b) ERC-7518 vs ERC-3643 is a fundamental architectural choice that shouldn't be defaulted. (c) Platform agnosticism is itself a competitive advantage — PleoChrome as the orchestration layer above any tokenization platform.
- **Impact:** Removed Brickken from landing page partners section. Removed all references to Brickken as the committed platform in public-facing materials. Internal evaluation continues in parallel.
- **Decided by:** Shane Pierson
