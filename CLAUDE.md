# PleoChrome — Project Rules

## What This Is

We are the orchestrator — we don't own, hold, or sell. We coordinate every specialist in the pipeline.

## Decision Audit Log
**CRITICAL RULE:** Every time a strategic decision, pivot, partner evaluation outcome, or structural change is made in conversation, you MUST:
1. Append the decision to `DECISION-AUDIT-LOG.md` in the repo root with: date, decision, rationale, impact, and who decided
2. Update ALL affected documents (strategy docs, portal pages, research files) to reflect the change
3. If the decision affects the Google Drive folder structure, update files there too

The audit log is the investor-facing record of how this company thinks. Every entry shows diligence, not indecision.

## Document Update Protocol
When updating any strategic document:
- Add a "Last Updated" header with date and summary of changes
- Never delete historical content — mark superseded sections with `[SUPERSEDED — see Decision #X]`
- Cross-reference the Decision Audit Log entry number

## Naming Conventions (Google Drive)
- Documents: `YYYY-MM-DD DESCRIPTION - Source.ext` (e.g., `2026-03-26 Meeting Notes - Zoniqx Intro Call.md`)
- DD Reports: `DD-Report-Entity-Name.md` or `.pdf`
- Strategic Reports: `Strategic-Partner-Report-Entity-Name.md` or `.pdf`
