## What is PleoChrome?

PleoChrome is a company that helps people turn expensive gemstones into investable financial products. We don't own the gems. We don't store them. We don't sell them to investors. We organize the entire process — every expert, every legal step, every compliance check — so that it gets done right.

Think of us like a general contractor for building a house. The plumber, electrician, architect, and inspector all do their jobs — but someone needs to coordinate them, make sure they show up in the right order, and verify every step meets code. That's what PleoChrome does for gemstone-backed financial products.

## What's the Problem We Solve?

High-value gemstones (emeralds, rubies, sapphires) are worth billions globally, but they're almost impossible to invest in. You can't buy a fraction of an emerald. Banks won't lend against them easily. There's no stock exchange for gemstones. The market is opaque, illiquid, and unregulated.

PleoChrome fixes this by creating a regulated, transparent, institutional-grade process that turns a physical gemstone into something investors can actually participate in.

## Three Ways to Create Value from One Gemstone

### 1. Fractional Securities
**What it means:** Split the gemstone into smaller shares that multiple investors can buy.
**How it works:** We create a legal entity (SPV) that owns the gem. Investors buy shares of that entity. Think of it like buying a share of a house instead of the whole house.
**Who it's for:** Investors who want exposure to gemstones at lower minimums.
**Regulation:** SEC Reg D 506(c) — accredited investors only, general advertising permitted.

### 2. Tokenization
**What it means:** Represent the gemstone's value as digital tokens on a blockchain.
**How it works:** After the gem is certified, appraised, and vaulted, we issue security tokens (like digital stock certificates) that live on a blockchain. These tokens have compliance rules built into the code — so only approved investors can hold or trade them.
**Who it's for:** Investors who want digital ownership with potential for 24/7 trading.
**Regulation:** Same SEC rules, but with programmable compliance enforced by smart contracts.

### 3. Debt Instruments (Lending)
**What it means:** Use the gemstone as collateral for a loan.
**How it works:** The gem owner puts their stone in a vault, and we arrange a loan against it — similar to how you'd borrow against a house. The owner keeps the gem, but can't touch it until the loan is repaid. If they default, the lender can sell the gem.
**Who it's for:** Gem owners who want cash now without selling their asset. And lenders who want predictable yields backed by a real physical asset.
**Regulation:** UCC Article 9 secured transactions, state lending regulations.

## How the Process Works (Any Path)

Every gemstone goes through the same verification pipeline regardless of which financial product we create:

1. **We verify who owns it** — Background checks, sanctions screening, provenance verification
2. **We prove what it is** — GIA lab certification (the gold standard in gemology)
3. **We determine what it's worth** — Three independent appraisals by certified professionals. We use the average of the two lowest — so valuations are always conservative.
4. **We put it in a real vault** — Institutional-grade storage (Brink's-level), insured, with real-time verification
5. **We handle all the legal paperwork** — Securities counsel drafts the offering documents, files with the SEC, handles state registrations
6. **We create the financial product** — Whether that's fractional shares, digital tokens, or a loan agreement
7. **We connect it to investors or lenders** — Onboarding, verification, and compliance for everyone involved
