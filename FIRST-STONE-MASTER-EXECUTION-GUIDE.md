### BLOCKER 1: THE EMIEL KANDI SITUATION

**What the research revealed:**

Emiel A. Kandi is a convicted federal felon. In April 2014, he pleaded guilty in U.S. District Court (Western District of Washington, Case No. CR13-5369-RBL) to Conspiracy to Submit False Statements in Loan Applications and Making False Statements to HUD. He was sentenced to five years in federal prison and ordered to pay $831,000+ in restitution. The fraud involved falsifying employment, salary, and occupancy information on at least 19 home mortgage loan applications.

The emeralds themselves have a convoluted history. White Oak Partners II LLC was incorporated in 2008 as a subsidiary of Strategic Worldwide Investments and received the barrels of emeralds in 2010. Court documents reveal that a bank loan officer (Jeff Goodell, also convicted of bank fraud -- stealing $1M from the Tacoma Rescue Mission) had faked loan forms for an $8.716 million line of credit for White Oak, to be secured by "four barrels of uncut emeralds worth $47 million." Two former associates allegedly changed the company's state records to make themselves managers in early 2011, then had the emeralds moved from Tacoma to a vault in Florida without Kandi's permission. In 2016, Kandi transferred all membership shares of the company to his own name. In 2019, he obtained a default judgment in Pierce County Superior Court declaring himself the owner. However, that same year probation officers filed a petition accusing him of being self-employed without permission and failing to disclose all assets -- both violations of his federal supervision conditions.

**Why this is CRITICAL for PleoChrome:**

1. Any SEC-regulated securities offering associated with a convicted financial felon will face extreme scrutiny
2. Investors performing due diligence will discover Kandi immediately -- it is front-page news in the Tacoma News Tribune and indexed in federal court records
3. The ownership chain is murky: White Oak Partners II LLC -> Strategic Worldwide Investments -> Kandi (via a default judgment where defendants didn't respond -- one was in state prison for homicide)
4. There may be outstanding federal restitution obligations that could create liens on Kandi's assets
5. Any broker-dealer or institutional partner will flag this in compliance review

**Resolution path (discuss with securities counsel immediately):**

- **Option A (Preferred): Clean separation.** PleoChrome does NOT deal with Kandi directly. The stone owner/entity must be a clean, verifiable party. If the stones are owned by an entity (e.g., an LLC with clean ownership), PleoChrome deals with that entity. Kandi personally should not appear anywhere in the offering documents, marketing, or investor-facing materials. Securities counsel must opine on whether the provenance chain is clean enough for a Reg D offering.
- **Option B: Full disclosure with enhanced DD.** If there is no way to separate Kandi from the chain of custody, every piece of the history must be fully disclosed in the PPM risk factors. This will make the offering harder to sell but is legally defensible.
- **Option C: Walk away.** If securities counsel determines the provenance risk is too high, this particular barrel should not be PleoChrome's first deal. The first deal needs to be pristine.
- **Specific actions:**
  1. Engage securities counsel BEFORE any further contact with Kandi or the vault
  2. Run a comprehensive background check including PACER federal court records, Washington SOS, OFAC SDN, FinCEN, all 50-state criminal records
  3. Determine if there are any outstanding liens, restitution obligations, or supervised release conditions that could affect asset disposition
  4. Verify the 2019 Pierce County default judgment is final and uncontested
  5. Determine the current legal status of White Oak Partners II LLC
  6. Get a legal opinion letter specifically addressing the provenance chain

### BLOCKER 3: LEGAL ENTITY FORMATION

**What:** Form PleoChrome Holdings LLC as a Wyoming Series LLC

**Steps:**
1. File Articles of Organization at wyobiz.wyo.gov -- $100 filing fee
2. Engage a Wyoming registered agent -- $50-$200/year (Northwest Registered Agent, Wyoming Agents, etc.)
3. Draft Operating Agreement (securities counsel should do this -- $2,000-$5,000)
4. Obtain EIN from IRS -- free, online at irs.gov, takes 5 minutes
5. Open business bank account (need EIN + Articles)
6. Register for Wyoming annual report -- $60/year minimum (due on anniversary month)

**Why Wyoming specifically:**
- Series LLC statute allows creating separate series for each asset without filing new entities
- Each series has its own assets, liabilities, members -- legally segregated
- Only the parent files the annual report and pays the fee
- Digital asset-friendly state with progressive blockchain legislation
- No state income tax

**Timeline:** 3-5 business days for filing, 1-2 weeks total with bank account.
**Cost:** ~$400-$600 for formation + first year registered agent.

## PART 6: SUCCESS METRICS

### Stage-by-Stage "Done" Criteria

**Phase 0 (Foundation) is DONE when:**
- PleoChrome Holdings LLC is formed with EIN, bank account, and operating agreement
- Securities counsel is engaged with a signed letter of engagement
- A compliance officer is designated and AML/KYC policy is drafted
- Insurance is bound (D&O, E&O, Cyber, GL at minimum)
- Legal opinion on Kandi/provenance is received and decision made (proceed or walk away)
- Vault bill is resolved with written confirmation

**Phase 1 (Asset Intake) is DONE when:**
- Asset holder has completed the full intake questionnaire with all attachments
- KYC/KYB verification is passed
- OFAC, PEP, and sanctions screenings are all CLEAR
- Initial provenance review is documented
- Engagement agreement is signed by both parties
- No red flags exist or all red flags have been formally addressed

**Phase 2 (Certification & Valuation) is DONE when:**
- All significant stones have GIA Identification & Origin Reports, verified online
- SSEF origin determination is complete (if applicable)
- All 3 independent USPAP-compliant appraisals are received
- Variance analysis shows acceptable spread (<15-20%)
- Final offering value is determined and formally documented

**Phase 3 (Custody) is DONE when:**
- Stones are in an institutional-grade vault with segregated storage
- Vault Receipt / Custody Acknowledgment is in hand
- Vault insurance covers the full appraised value (confirmed in writing)
- API/reporting feed is active (or manual verification process is established)

**Phase 4 (Legal Structuring) is DONE when:**
- Series A SPV is formed under the master LLC
- PPM, Subscription Agreement, Token Purchase Agreement, and Custody Agreement are all finalized and reviewed by compliance
- MSB legal opinion is received and any required registrations are filed
- Form D is prepared (ready to file within 15 days of first sale)

**Phase 5 (Tokenization) is DONE when:**
- Token is deployed on Polygon mainnet with correct parameters
- All compliance rules are active and tested (KYC whitelist, accreditation check, jurisdiction blocks)
- Chainlink PoR feed is live and verified (or manual fallback is operational)
- Smart contract audit is passed with no critical/high findings
- Token parameters match legal documents exactly (independently verified)

**Phase 6 (Launch) is DONE when:**
- Form D is filed with SEC
- Blue sky notices are filed in all applicable states
- Investor onboarding pathway is live and tested end-to-end
- At least one test investor has successfully completed the full flow
- Marketing materials are compliant and approved by counsel
- Data room is live with all documentation

**Phase 7 (Distribution) -- "First Stone Success" means:**
- At least 3 investors have successfully purchased tokens
- Total capital raised exceeds $1M (demonstrates market validation)
- All investor KYC/accreditation records are properly maintained
- Quarterly reporting schedule is established
- Annual re-appraisal calendar is set
- No SEC, FINRA, or FinCEN enforcement actions or inquiries
- Chainlink PoR feed is operational and verified
- PleoChrome has collected its setup fee and first annual admin fees

### Ultimate Success Indicator

The first stone is fully successful when:
1. The offering is fully subscribed (100% of tokens sold)
2. All regulatory filings are current and compliant
3. Investor reporting is running on schedule
4. The process has been documented well enough to become the template for Stone #2
5. At least two institutional partners (vault, BD, auditor) have expressed interest in handling additional assets
6. PleoChrome has generated sufficient revenue from fees to cover ongoing operating costs
