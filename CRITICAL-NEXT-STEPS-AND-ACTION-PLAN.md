### RED FLAG TRIGGERS (Stop and Reassess)

If any of these occur, immediately pause and convene an emergency team meeting:

| Red Flag | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Securities counsel advises that Kandi provenance makes the offering legally indefensible | STOP first asset work. Pivot to alternative asset. Structural work (entity, insurance, counsel) is not wasted. |
| Federal restitution lien discovered on Kandi's assets (including the emeralds) | STOP. Emeralds may be encumbered. Counsel must opine on whether lien can be resolved before proceeding. |
| GIA reports reveal stones are significantly lower quality than represented | Reassess offering value. May still proceed at lower valuation. Set investor expectations accordingly. |
| Appraisal variance exceeds 25% | Commission 4th tiebreaker appraisal. Investigate the outlier. Do NOT average and proceed blindly. |
| MSB legal opinion says PleoChrome IS an MSB requiring state money transmitter licenses | Budget $500K+ for 47-state licensing, or restructure operations so all fund flows go through BD/SPV, not PleoChrome. |
| Smart contract audit reveals critical vulnerability | DO NOT deploy to mainnet. Fix, re-audit, re-test. Delay timeline by 2-4 weeks. |
| Brickken goes offline or materially changes terms | Activate contingency: maintain all contract ABIs and deployment records. Evaluate Tokeny, Securitize, or direct ERC-3643 deployment as alternatives. |
| Investor or BD discover Kandi history and refuse to participate | This was anticipated. Ensure PPM disclosure is ironclad. If pattern of refusals emerges, reassess the first asset. |
| Team member departure | Invoke Operating Agreement provisions. Cross-train on all processes immediately. No single point of failure. |
| Cash reserves drop below $30,000 before first investor close | Emergency bridge needed. Options: personal capital call, friends-and-family loan, delay non-essential spend. |
