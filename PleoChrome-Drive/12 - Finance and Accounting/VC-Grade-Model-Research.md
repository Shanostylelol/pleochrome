## Key Frameworks

### a16z's 13 Marketplace Metrics

### HBS POCD Framework
People, Opportunity, Context, Deal — degree of fit among all four determines viability

### Key Benchmarks
- LTV:CAC > 3x (Series A), > 5x (growth)
- CAC Payback: 5-12 months
- Gross Margin > 60%
- NRR > 120% (best-in-class)
- Monthly Churn < 2%
- Take Rate: 10-30% typical marketplace
- Burn Multiple < 1.5x (efficient)
- Rule of 40: revenue growth % + profit margin % > 40%

### Wall Street Color Conventions
- Blue font = hard-coded inputs
- Black font = formulas (same sheet)
- Green font = cross-sheet references
- Red font = external data
- Yellow shading = input cells
- Light blue shading = calculated results
- Negatives in parentheses: (1,234) not -1,234

### What Kills Credibility
- "1% of TAM" fallacy
- Hockey stick without mechanism
- No unit economics
- False precision in out-years
- No scenario analysis
- Cash timing blindness
- Math errors

### RWA-Specific Investor Concerns
1. Regulatory risk (Reg D, MiCA, VARA)
2. Custody risk (who holds the physical asset?)
3. Liquidity risk (will tokens trade?)
4. Valuation risk (pricing unique assets)
5. Oracle risk (proving asset still exists)
6. Insurance coverage adequacy
